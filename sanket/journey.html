Our Journey of Impact

From vision to reality: How SANKET has evolved over the years

Since our founding, SANKET has been on a mission to create meaningful change in communities across the nation. What began as a small initiative by a group of passionate individuals has grown into a movement that has touched thousands of lives.

Our journey has been marked by challenges, breakthroughs, and countless moments of transformation. Through it all, we have remained committed to our core belief: that true change begins within each of us.

Explore our timeline below to see how SANKET has evolved, the milestones we've achieved, and the impact we've created together with our communities, volunteers, and partners.

Timeline of Achievements

Key milestones in our journey of creating change

2010

The Beginning

SANKET was founded by a group of five friends with a shared vision of community empowerment through education and skill development.

2012

First Community Center

Opened our first community center in Delhi, offering free educational support and vocational training to underprivileged youth.

2014

Rural Outreach Program

Launched our rural outreach program, extending SANKET's impact to 10 villages in northern India through mobile education units.

2016

National Recognition

Received the National Community Service Award for our innovative approach to education and skill development in underserved communities.

2018

Digital Literacy Initiative

Launched our Digital Literacy Initiative, providing technology access and training to over 5,000 individuals across 25 communities.

2020

Pandemic Response

Pivoted our programs to address the COVID-19 crisis, providing emergency relief, health education, and remote learning support to affected communities.

2021

Environmental Sustainability Program

Launched our Environmental Sustainability Program, focusing on community-led conservation efforts and sustainable practices training.

2022

Expansion to 5 States

Expanded our operations to 5 states across India, with 15 community centers and a network of over 500 dedicated volunteers.

2023

Today & Beyond

Continuing our mission with renewed focus on sustainable development, digital inclusion, and creating self-reliant communities across India.

Key Achievements

Highlights from our journey of impact

2015-2020

Education for All Initiative

Provided quality education to over 5,000 children from underprivileged backgrounds, with 85% continuing to higher education.

2016-Present

Women Empowerment Program

Trained 3,000+ women in vocational skills, with 70% establishing their own micro-enterprises or securing employment.

2018-Present

Community Health Initiative

Conducted 200+ health camps serving over 10,000 individuals with free health check-ups, medicines, and health education.

2021-Present

Green Communities Project

Planted 25,000+ trees and established 15 community gardens, promoting environmental sustainability and food security.

2019-Present

Youth Leadership Academy

Mentored 500+ young leaders who have gone on to initiate their own community development projects.

2020-Present

Digital Inclusion Program

Established 10 digital learning centers providing technology access and skills to underserved communities.
