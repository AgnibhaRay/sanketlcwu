Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with SANKET?

You can volunteer with SANKET by visiting our Volunteering page and filling out the registration form. We offer various volunteering opportunities based on your skills, interests, and availability.

How can I donate to SANKET?

You can donate to SANKET through our website's donation page. We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI. All donations are tax-deductible under Section 80G.

Can I partner with SANKET for CSR activities?

Yes, SANKET welcomes corporate partnerships for CSR initiatives. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@sanket.org to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

How can I stay updated on SANKET's activities?

You can stay updated on our activities by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media, or regularly visiting our website's Events and News sections.

Does SANKET offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we offer internship opportunities for students and young professionals in various fields. Please send your resume and a cover letter to volunteer@sanket.org with the subject line "Internship Application".
