Join Our Events

Connect, learn, and make a difference with SANKET

SANKET organizes a variety of events throughout the year, from educational workshops and community service initiatives to fundraisers and awareness campaigns. These events provide opportunities for learning, networking, and making a positive impact in our communities.

Whether you're interested in volunteering, developing new skills, or simply connecting with like-minded individuals, our events offer something for everyone. Browse our upcoming events below and join us in creating meaningful change.

Featured Event

Annual Fundraising Gala: Illuminating Futures

Join us for an elegant evening celebrating SANKET's achievements and raising funds for our educational initiatives. The gala will feature inspiring stories from our beneficiaries, live performances, a silent auction, and a gourmet dinner.

Your participation will directly support our scholarship program, which provides educational opportunities to underprivileged children across India.
