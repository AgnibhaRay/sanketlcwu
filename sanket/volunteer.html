Why Volunteer With Us?

Make a difference while growing personally and professionally

Volunteering with SANKET is more than just giving your time—it's about becoming part of a movement that creates meaningful change from within. Our volunteers are the backbone of our organization, bringing diverse skills, perspectives, and passion to our mission.

Whether you can commit a few hours a week or join us for specific projects, your contribution matters. We offer a range of volunteering opportunities that match your interests, skills, and availability, ensuring that your experience is both impactful and fulfilling.

Join our community of change-makers and discover how giving back can transform not only the lives of others but also your own.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about volunteering with SANKET

What is the minimum time commitment required?

The time commitment varies depending on the volunteer role. Some positions require as little as 2-4 hours per week, while others may be project-based or event-specific. We value your time and try to be flexible, working with your schedule to find a suitable arrangement.

Do I need specific qualifications to volunteer?

While some roles may require specific skills or experience (such as teaching or healthcare backgrounds), many of our volunteer positions only require enthusiasm, dedication, and a willingness to learn. We provide training and support for all volunteers to ensure you feel confident in your role.

Can I volunteer remotely or do I need to be physically present?

We offer both in-person and remote volunteering opportunities. Roles such as content creation, social media management, and certain administrative tasks can be done remotely. However, many of our community-based programs require physical presence. Please specify your preference in your application.

What support will I receive as a volunteer?

All volunteers receive an orientation, role-specific training, and ongoing support from our volunteer coordinators. We also organize regular volunteer meetings and provide resources to help you succeed in your role. Additionally, we offer certificates of participation and reference letters for long-term volunteers.

How long does the application process take?

After submitting your application, you can expect to hear from us within 5-7 business days. The entire process, including interviews and orientation, typically takes 2-3 weeks. For urgent volunteer needs or event-specific volunteering, the process may be expedited.
